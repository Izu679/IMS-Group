Blossom Beauty

We have named our firm Blossom Beauty Sdn Bhd, and because of the exclusivity of our company's name, we are able to attract customers to our goods by utilizing an innovative brand. We're developing a skin-care product to raise awareness about how important it is to take proper care of one's skin, despite the fact that many people do not consider it to be an important organ in the body. This, on the other hand, is inaccurate.


Blossom Beauty is a leading Halal brand which consists of a unique and comprehensive range of skincare, personal care and toiletry products developed exclusively to meet the needs of modern Muslim women and men. Made with the finest natural ingredients that confirm to Syarak requirements and certified HALAL by a stringent independent body – JABATAN KEMAJUAN ISLAM MALAYSIA (JAKIM), Blossom Beauty is firmly steeped in Muslims and manufactured in ISO and “Good Manufacturing Practice” (GMP) certified production facilities. Blossom Beauty endeavors to provide the best quality products to meet the growing needs of our consumers. To ensure product quality, consistency and flexibility of supply, Blossom Beauty is manufactured 100% in factories located in Pengkalan Chepa, Kelantan.


The shade of our company logo is a combination of pink, white, black, and grey, reflecting our company identity as skin care experts. The combination colour of our logo symbolises that our product can be used for both women and men.

Our tagline is "Fall in Love with Your Skin", which means that we should love our skin and take care of it by using the correct skin care products to make it healthy. As we know, healthy skin reflects our beauty.

Vission


As a prominent professional brand with a strong presence in Malaysia, our guiding mission is to develop skin care products that provide our clients with unique results while also improving their overall quality of life. Continuous innovation targeted at the development of sophisticated skin care products based on active ingredients is a priority for the company. This is accomplished through the professional development and enrichment of human resources, as well as the provision of cosmeticians with knowledge and practical expertise.

Mission


At Blossom Beauty Sdn Bhd., we seek to manufacture and supply healthy and effective skin care products that are designed to treat, rejuvenate, and improve the skin and enhance our customers' looks, as well as improve their whole lifestyles by helping them seem attractive and regaining their confidence.
